Who are we?

A paramedic and an EMT with a passion for technology

Dan Bruce

Reliability engineer by day and paramedic by night, Dan came up with the initial idea for Aqua QA after becoming frustrated with paper QA sheets. He's been in EMS for four years and has loved (almost) every second of it. Find him singing country music in the flycar while driving to the call.

David “Sparky” Sparkman

Sparky started as just a driver—volunteer, collegiate EMS will do that to you. But he quickly learned that the back of the ambulance is the place to be. Although he seemingly always wrote software, he soon became a first lieutenant, in charge of stocking and vehicle maintenance. His day job is front-end developer, and we’re glad to have him doing the same thing here.
